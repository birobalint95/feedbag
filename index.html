How FeedBag.ai Works

Three simple steps to transform how you plan and execute strategy.

1

Kick-Off Your Roadmap

Already have a roadmap? That’s great! You don’t need to start over — simply connect with your existing tools and import your roadmap in seconds.

No roadmap yet? No problem — start from scratch effortlessly. With just a few quick inputs, we’ll generate an initial roadmap to get you going.

2

Enrich With Insights

Know your competitors. Forget research marathons — automatically spot competitor trends, monitor news and social channels, turning insights into clear battlecards and opportunities.

Spot trends before they go mainstream. Explore forums, community spaces and industry sources — highlighting popular discussions and rising trends before they peak.

Your team is heard and valued. Validate your roadmap by the people who execute it — automatically gather feedback, invite input on roadmap, and capture skills.

Engage customers effortlessly. A roadmap without customer input is just a guess - automatically collect feedback, interview stakeholders about their needs, and run polls or voting on deliveries.

3

Execute With Confidence

Keep your team always aligned. Dynamic roadmaps update automatically as new insights arrive, ensuring your team is always aligned, focused on the right priorities, and working from the latest information.

Make deliveries transparent for customers. Share specific parts of the roadmap with customers or users to provide clarity and visibility, so everyone knows what’s coming and can provide meaningful feedback.

Integrate changes seamlessly. Changes in your dynamic roadmap flow directly into your existing tools, keeping your workflows, task boards, and project management systems fully up to date without manual effort.

Export snapshots and generate reports. Easily export snapshots of the roadmap or generate detailed reports, transforming evolving plans into clear, shareable insights for your team and stakeholders.
